Bulk Apply Industry-Pair Template

Roll a published pair-template version (or a brand-new pair) onto many companies at once. Critical safety properties: per-company overrides are preserved by default, conflicts are surfaced before commit, the diff preview is per-company, and the audit log captures every individual edit. Use for re-baselining, onboarding a portfolio, or migrating companies onto a newly-published version.
