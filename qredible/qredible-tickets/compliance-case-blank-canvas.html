Designer notes — v1 cut decisions

Auto-title with override

Field is pre-filled with [Merchant] — [Date] so the analyst doesn't have to think about naming on the fast path.

The AUTO badge + ✎ Edit button signal it's editable — analysts who want to standardize ("Q1 Audit", "Quarterly Refresh") can override inline. Clearing the field re-applies the auto value.

No in-case picker (yet)

v1 doesn't ship the targeting picker or ⌘K quick-add. Joseph wants user feedback on the empty-draft + deep-link round-trip before designing the in-case picker.

Two buttons cover the actual sources: Add from Product Compliance (COA work) and Add from Q-ScanAI Findings (website / content). Each deep-links scoped to this merchant.

Stays DRAFT, round-trips cleanly

The case persists as DRAFT the moment it opens. Leaving doesn't lose anything.

The shipped per-row and bulk Add to case affordances (QP-3582 / QP-3581) attach to the draft. A confirmation toast deep-links back; if the analyst dismisses it, the draft is one click away from Q-Monitor → Cases → Drafts (N).

Send stays disabled until ≥ 1 item is attached.
