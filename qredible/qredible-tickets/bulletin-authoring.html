Policy-First New Surface (April 2026) — Bulletins are the user-facing output of policy changes. When a policy is published with a new required item, a changed severity, or an enforcement update, the team writes a bulletin explaining what changed and what merchants need to do. Bulletins are attached to one or more policy_ids and fan out to every merchant assigned to those policies. This ticket is net-new under the policy-first pivot (label: policy-first-new).

Why this ticket exists — Before the policy-first pivot there was no canonical way to tell merchants why a compliance requirement changed. Support handled it ad-hoc over email. Bulletins close that gap: every policy change that a merchant needs to act on ships with a matching bulletin, and the audience is computed from the same policy_ids that drive the rest of the engine.
