Problem

Bulk Unassign and Assign actions exist on the Products tab but not the COAs tab. Reviewers working COA-first cannot bulk-unassign without switching tabs, breaking their workflow.

Business Rules
BR-1Unassign removes product association from selected COAs. Selection must include at least one assigned COA.
BR-2Assign opens product selector. Selection must include at least one unassigned COA.
BR-3Both actions available in bulk action bar on checkbox selection.
BR-4Both actions logged in COA audit history with user and timestamp.
BR-5Mixed selection (assigned + unassigned) — both buttons shown; each applies only to the COAs where the action is valid.
Acceptance Criteria
☐COA rows in COAs tab selectable via checkbox
☐Bulk action bar appears on any checkbox selection
☐Unassign removes product association from all selected assigned COAs with confirmation
☐Assign opens product selector, assigns all selected unassigned COAs to chosen product
☐Both actions logged in COA audit history
☐Table refreshes with correct updated state after action
☐Mixed selection handled per BR-5
